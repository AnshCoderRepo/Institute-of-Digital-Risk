# Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) — Landing Page Assessment

A premium, high-performance landing page developed for the **Institute of Digital Risk (IDR)**. This project demonstrates modern web development practices using pure Vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, designed to reflect IDR's mission of bridging academic excellence with industry-led digital risk practice.

## 🚀 Overview

This website serves as the primary digital touchpoint for IDR, clearly articulating their value proposition: training and deploying the next generation of digital risk practitioners. The design is tailored for highly regulated, high-consequence environments such as financial services and critical national infrastructure.

## ✨ Key Features

### 1. Mandatory Sections Implemented:
- **Hero Section**: Impactful mission statement with call-to-action for programs.
- **About IDR**: Detailed breakdown of the institute's industry-led model and UK university partnerships.
- **Three Pillars Model**: Interactive cards showcasing the **Academy**, **Innovation & Incubation**, and **Advisory Services**.
- **The IDR Pipeline**: A visual representation of the "Train – Hire – Innovate – Deploy" lifecycle.
- **Community/Sectors**: Clear identification of stakeholders (students, practitioners, organizations) and target industries.
- **Contact/Registration**: A validated interest-registration form with a custom success state.

### 2. Technical Excellence:
- **Semantic HTML5**: Native elements used throughout to ensure SEO optimization and accessibility.
- **Custom Design System**: A "Cyber-Industrial" aesthetic built from scratch using a dark palette and high-contrast orange (`#FF6B00`) accents.
- **Responsive Engineering**: Fully fluid layout using **CSS Grid** and **Flexbox**—optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop viewports.
- **Performance-First JavaScript**:
  - **Intersection Observer API**: For smooth scroll-reveal animations that improve engagement without taxing the CPU.
  - **Native Smooth Scrolling**: Enhanced with a JS fallback for legacy browser support.
  - **State Management**: Zero-dependency logic for mobile menu toggles and form handling.

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

- **Markup**: Semantic HTML5
- **Styling**: Vanilla CSS3 (Custom Properties / Flexbox / Grid)
- **Logics**: Vanilla JavaScript (ES6+)
- **Typography**: Google Fonts (Bebas Neue, Source Serif 4, DM Mono)
- **Assets**: Inline SVGs for optimized loading and resolution independence.

## 🎨 Design & Brand Identity

### 💠 Visual Philosophy
The IDR visual identity is crafted to project authority, innovation, and resilience. The design departs from traditional corporate aesthetics to reflect a modern, tech-forward institution.

- **Color Palette**: Uses a striking contrast of **Burnt Orange (#FF6B00)** and **Ink Black**. This combination is energetic yet authoritative, providing a vibrant, professional feel that stands out from typical industry standards.
- **Typography**: Features clean, minimalist sans-serif typography for brand clarity. This alignment supports IDR's identity as a cutting-edge, educational, and tech-focused entity.
- **Symbolism**: The **abstract cube graphic** serves as a core visual metaphor. It represents stability and the interconnected nature of digital systems, visually grounding the concepts of risk management and architectural resilience.
