The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute purpose-built for the demands of digital, cyber, and technology risk in regulated, high-consequence environments. We bridge the gap between academic knowledge and operational practice — equipping practitioners with the skills that sectors like financial services and critical national infrastructure actually need.

Our programmes are developed in close partnership with leading UK universities, ensuring that every bootcamp, qualification pathway, and advisory engagement is grounded in rigorous research as well as lived industry experience. We don't just produce qualified graduates — we produce deployable professionals.

From emerging AI governance to established frameworks like ISO 27001, NIST, and NIS2, IDR operates at the intersection of regulation, technology and human capability — precisely where the risk landscape is evolving fastest.

Who We
Serve

IDR was built for practitioners who operate where the stakes are highest. Our community spans students entering the profession for the first time through to senior risk leaders navigating the complexities of emerging technology regulation.

We serve individuals and organisations across the full spectrum of digital and cyber risk — with a particular focus on regulated environments where accountability, resilience, and governance cannot be an afterthought.
